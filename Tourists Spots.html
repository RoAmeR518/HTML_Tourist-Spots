Metropolitan Cathedral of
Our Lord's Transfiguration

The Metropolitan Cathedral of Our Lord's Transfiguration, also known as Palo Metropolitan Cathedral or simply Palo Cathedral, is a Roman Catholic church located at Palo, Leyte in the Philippines belonging to the Vicariate of Palo under the Metropolitan Archdiocese of Palo.

Monte Cueva
(Maasin, Southern Leyte)

Monte Cueva shrine is a place that is well-known for the Marian devotees. This place has been a must-see place for the devotees and the pilgrims. As for its origin, it all started with a dream. The owner of the land who happens to be a lawyer, Joaquin Chung Jr., dreamt of the Lady of the Assumption asking him to put up a shrine in that specific area. He believed in his dream, and so he right away built the church and eventually gave it to the ownership and management of the Diocese.

Sambawan Island

Sambawan Island is Biliran's answer to paradise. White sands, thriving marine life and warm blue waters lapping up the tree-lined shore is a must for those wanting to escape from reality. Sambawan Island is located 5km of the west coast of Maripipi Municipality, south of the Samar sea, is fast becoming the most visited attraction by the tourists. The semi-curved beach with soaring rock formations covered with green grass as a backdrop create a bubble of fantasy that you are entering another dimension.

Bangon falls

This is a must go to place if you are in Calbayog. The trip to the falls require taking a ride on a motor bike called locally as a Habal-Habal. Its about 3 km of rough and bumpy rock like terrain. Then you walk along the river bed to the three layer waterfalls and can even climb all the way up to the top lake and take a swim. This should be done on the summer months so the climb is not to difficult or the cascades too strong.

San Bernardino Strait

The San Bernardino Strait is a strait in the Philippines, connecting the Samar Sea with the Philippine Sea. It separates the Bicol Peninsula of Luzon island from the island of Samar in the south.

Tubabao

Tubabao is an island off the southeastern point of Samar island. It is one of the islands comprising the town of Guiuan in the province of Eastern samar, in east central Philippines.
